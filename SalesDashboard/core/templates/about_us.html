About 1capital.in

Your trusted partner in wealth management and investment advisory since 2000. We combine expertise, transparency, and innovation to help you achieve your financial goals.

Who We Are

1capital.in is a SEBI-registered research and investment advisory firm dedicated to providing transparent, data-driven financial solutions. With over 20 years of collective industry experience, our team of expert analysts combines macro-economic insights with sector-specific expertise to deliver superior investment strategies across equities, mutual funds, AIFs, and portfolio management services.

Our Investment Philosophy

"We don't chase past performance - we analyze where markets are heading."

Our philosophy is built on the foundation that past performance is not indicative of future results. Instead, we focus on forward-looking analysis, understanding macroeconomic trends, identifying sector rotation opportunities, and positioning portfolios to capture emerging market movements. This proactive approach allows us to help our clients stay ahead of market cycles and optimize their investment returns.
