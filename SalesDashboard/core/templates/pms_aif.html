Portfolio Management Services (PMS) and Alternative Investment Funds (AIF) designed for wealth creation and capital preservation. Professionally managed strategies tailored to your unique financial objectives.

Portfolio Management Services (PMS)

PMS represents a personalized approach to wealth management. Rather than following index-based strategies, we create customized portfolios that align with your specific financial goals, risk appetite, and time horizon.

What is PMS?

  • Professionally Managed Portfolios

    Customized equity portfolios designed specifically for your goals and investment horizon.

  • Goal-Aligned Strategy

    Aligned to your goals, risk profile, and investment horizon with personalized recommendations.

  • Active Management Approach

    Active investment approach driven by market analysis and macro economic trends.

Why Choose 1capital.in for PMS?

  • Strong In-House Research

    Deep market intelligence and proprietary research capabilities.

  • Forward-Looking Strategy

    Focused on where markets are heading - not driven by past performance charts.

  • Data-Driven Decisions

    Decisions based on valuations, sector cycles, and economic indicators.

  • Dynamic Rebalancing

    Portfolio adjustments as market conditions evolve for optimal positioning.

  • Transparent Reporting

    Clear advisory support and regular performance reporting.

  • Risk-Adjusted Returns

    Focus on risk-adjusted returns, not just absolute returns.

PMS Minimum Investment

INR50 Lakhs

Ideal for high-net-worth individuals seeking professional equity management with direct ownership of individual securities and customized portfolio structure.

Alternative Investment Funds (AIF)

Alternative Investment Funds offer investors exposure to investment strategies and asset classes beyond traditional equity and fixed income. AIFs provide opportunities for portfolio diversification and alpha generation through specialized investment approaches.

What are AIFs?

  • Beyond Traditional Investments

    Investment products beyond traditional equity and fixed income securities.

  • Diverse Strategies

    Includes private credit, hedge strategies, structured equity, and other specialized approaches.

  • Diversification & Alpha

    Built for diversification and alpha generation across your investment portfolio.

Why Choose 1capital.in for AIF?

  • Deep Due Diligence

    Thorough analysis of fund strategy and manager capability.

  • Cyclic Opportunity Selection

    Selection based on economic cycles and thematic opportunities.

  • Portfolio Complementarity

    Allocation designed to complement your existing portfolios.

  • Independent Recommendations

    Unbiased advice - no product pushing, only what fits your needs.

  • Clear Communication

    Simplified explanation of complex strategies for better decision-making.

AIF Minimum Investment

INR1.0 Crore

Designed for ultra-high-net-worth individuals and institutions seeking exposure to alternative strategies including private equity, hedge funds, structured products, and other specialized investment vehicles.

PMS vs AIF Comparison

Feature Portfolio Management Services (PMS) Alternative Investment Funds (AIF)
Asset Class Primary focus on equities with customization options Non-traditional assets, hedge strategies, private credit, etc.
Ownership Direct ownership of individual securities in your name Units in the pooled AIF fund
Management Active, personalized management for individual client Professional management with defined investment strategy
Flexibility Highly customizable based on individual preferences Standardized strategy across all investors
Minimum Investment INR50 Lakhs INR1.0 Crore
Liquidity Generally higher liquidity with daily valuations Lower liquidity with defined lock-in periods
Time Horizon Medium to long-term (3-5 years) Long-term (5+ years)
Best For Investors seeking active equity management with customization Investors seeking diversification beyond traditional assets
