What are Mutual Funds?

Mutual Funds pool money from multiple investors and invest it in a diversified portfolio of stocks, bonds, or other securities. They are managed by professional fund managers, offering an easy way to invest in financial markets without needing large capital or expertise.

Investment Categories

  • Equity Funds: Focused on stock market investments for growth potential
  • Debt Funds: Primarily invested in bonds and fixed-income securities
  • Hybrid Funds: Balanced blend of equity and debt for moderate growth

Advantages of Mutual Funds

  • Professional Management: Managed by experienced experts
  • Diversification: Spread risk across multiple assets
  • Affordable Investing: Start with small SIP amounts
  • Liquidity: Easy buy and redemption options
  • Transparency: Regular updates and disclosures
  • Tax Benefits: ELSS funds help save tax
